Corporate branding is the process of maximising all business assets to provide a powerful message.

Why would you do it? Or what’s in it for me?

There are a number of reasons it is a very powerful exercise:

  1. Corporate branding ensures that the company delivers the same message. In simplistic sense this ends with a slogan or sales line. The process of creating this simple sales statement is a result of research and evaluation of the business to identify:

    1. Demographic analysis of the existing client base – ensuring the business is a reflection of their requirements
    2. Research into the market to find a direction for the company that will ensure long term growth and profitability. An ancient proverb, “If you don’t have a goal, how can you know when you’ve arrived?” is a great summary of this process.
  2. It ensures the company is presented in the same way regardless of the media type ensuring a consistence delivery of the corporate message. Ensuring we engage both conscious and subconscious processes the effect is to leave a lasting impression on future clients.

How?

There are a number of ways this can be achieved and this is dependent on the current state of the business. For some companies the process requires a fundamental shift, while for others it’s the recreation of the visual and written materials to clarify goals and perception. We specialise in enhancing online presence so that your business is easily found, quickly recognised with a strong corporate message. By assessing your intended audience we ensure your site matches their expectation increasing feelings of assurance and desirability
